This website is a simple web application for the final project of course 2XB3.

Users can search for historical occurrences of earthquakes near their current location.

The earthquake data is from Canada Government Open datasets

You can search the historial earthquakes in the Search page. You can enter the coordinates of your target location or get the coordinates of your current location from the map. When you click search, it will show you the detail information of historial earthquakes within the given radius.
